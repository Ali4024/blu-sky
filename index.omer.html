Premium Economy

Premium Economy class offers a significant upgrade from the standard Economy class,providing passengers with enhanced comfort and amenities.

Here are some of the key characteristics of Premium Economy class:

-Additional Legroom: Typically around 38 inches of seat pitch, compared to 31-32 inches in Economy1.

-Adjustable Headrests and Legrests: For added comfort during the flight

-Larger Entertainment Screens: Providing a better viewing experience1.

-Power and USB Sockets: To keep electronic devices charged1.

-Enhanced Dining Options: Premium Economy often includes a separate food menu and improved meal service2.

-Additional Perks: Such as priority boarding, increased baggage allowance.
